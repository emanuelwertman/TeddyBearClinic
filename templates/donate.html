Support Our Mission

Help us bring smiles and healthcare education to children

Why Your Support Matters

The Teddy Bear Clinic transforms how children view healthcare and emergency services. Your donation directly impacts the 250+ children we serve each year in Morris County, helping them overcome fears and build positive connections with first responders, doctors, and community helpers.

Every child deserves to feel safe and confident when visiting a doctor or meeting a police officer or firefighter. Your generosity helps us provide free, interactive experiences where children bring their teddy bears for "check-ups" while learning about health, wellness, and safety in a comforting, playful environment. Together, we're building a generation that trusts and respects the heroes in our community.

Your Impact

Making a Difference Together

Since our inaugural event in 2025, the Teddy Bear Clinic has welcomed over 250 children and their families, partnering with Denville Police, Fire, EMS departments, and local hospitals to create unforgettable learning experiences.

Your donations help us bring together community heroes and young learners in ways that break down barriers and build trust. Children who once feared doctor visits or were intimidated by sirens now wave excitedly at fire trucks and look forward to their check-ups.

As we expand our reach across Morris County and beyond, your support ensures that more children can experience the magic of learning through play, compassion, and community connection.

Ways to Give

One-Time Donation

Make an immediate impact with a one-time gift of any amount. Whether $10 or $1,000, every dollar helps provide teddy bears, educational supplies, and memorable experiences for children in our community.

Monthly Giving

Join our Circle of Care with a recurring monthly donation. Consistent support helps us plan bigger events, reach more families, and expand our programs throughout the year.

In-Kind Donations

Donate new teddy bears, first aid supplies, educational materials, or event supplies. We also welcome volunteers and professional services to help make our events extraordinary.

How Your Donations Help

Teddy Bears & Supplies

Provide cuddly companions for children who need one, plus bandages, stickers, and comfort items for teddy bear "treatments."

First Aid Kits

Equip children with take-home first aid kits so they can continue learning about health and safety with their families.

Educational Materials

Fund coloring books, activity sheets, and interactive learning tools that teach children about healthcare and emergency services.

Event Operations

Support venue setup, refreshments, signage, and logistics that make our free community events possible for all families.

Ready to Make a Difference?

Join us in creating a world where every child approaches healthcare and emergency services with confidence, curiosity, and trust. Your generosity helps us continue our mission of bringing free, interactive healthcare education to children across Morris County and beyond.
