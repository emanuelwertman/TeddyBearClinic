Frequently Asked Questions

How can I register my child for an event?

You can register for Teddy Bear Clinic 2026 through our official registration page. Visit the Events section or click the Register button to sign up for upcoming events.

What is the Teddy Bear Clinic?

The Teddy Bear Clinic is an interactive community event that brings together local organizations—including police, fire, EMS, and hospitals—to educate children and families while connecting them to important community resources.

How can I start a Teddy Bear Clinic chapter at my school?

Starting a chapter is more than an event—it's a movement! DM us on Instagram @theteddybearclinic with any questions or if you want to learn more about bringing TBC to your community.

Where is the event held?

Our main event is held at Morris County School of Technology (MCST) in Denville, NJ. We welcome children from across Morris County and beyond!
