Who We Are

More Than Just A Clinic

The Teddy Bear Clinic is an interactive community event designed to ease children's fears around healthcare and emergency services. By bringing together local first responders, medical professionals, and educators, we create a safe, playful environment where children can learn and explore.

What started as a student project has grown into a community-wide initiative serving hundreds of families in Morris County.
