Our Story

The journey of bringing smiles and healthcare education to children

How It All Began

Hi, we are The Teddy Bear Clinic!

In May of 2025, the Academy of Education & Learning at MCST had the opportunity to host the inaugural Morris County Teddy Bear Clinic, an interactive community event that welcomed over 250 children.

The clinic brought together local organizationsâ€”including the Denville Police, Fire, and EMS departments, as well as hospitals in the areaâ€”to educate children and their families and connect them to community resources.

Children were invited to bring their favorite stuffed animals or teddy bears for a "check-up," allowing them to engage with community helpers in a comforting, playful, and non-threatening environment. The comforting presence of a teddy bear helps ease children's fears surrounding doctor visits, first responders, and unfamiliar environments.

The Teddy Bear Clinic aims to foster a better understanding of health, wellness, and safety in a supportive and engaging way.

Our Journey

2024

The Beginning

The Teddy Bear Clinic was founded at Morris County School of Technology (MCST) as part of the Academy of Education and Learning program.

2025

First Major Event

Our first Teddy Bear Clinic event welcomed over 250 children from across Morris County, partnering with Denville Police, Fire, EMS, and local hospitals.

2025

Growing Recognition

We presented to superintendents and administrators across New Jersey at NJASCD about how community events redefine student success.

2025

Learning & Development

Our team visited Liberty Science Center to learn how to effectively use inquiry in activities at the Teddy Bear Clinic and in the classroom.

2026

Expanding Impact

Teddy Bear Clinic 2026 is scheduled for May 3rd, with plans to expand both in size and impact across more communities.
