Teddy Bear Clinic 2025

Morris County School of Technology

Our inaugural Teddy Bear Clinic was a huge success! Over 250 children attended with their stuffed animals to learn about healthcare and meet local first responders. The event featured teddy bear check-ups, fire truck tours, EMS demonstrations, and arts & crafts stations.

2025 Event Highlights

250+ Children

Over 250 children participated in our first annual event, bringing their favorite stuffed animals for check-ups.

Healthcare Education

Kids learned about basic health concepts through interactive teddy bear examinations and demonstrations.

Fire Department

Local firefighters brought trucks and taught children about fire safety in an engaging way.

EMS Services

Children toured ambulances and learned what EMTs do to help people in emergencies.

Police Department

Officers connected with kids, showing them police vehicles and explaining how they keep communities safe.

Arts & Crafts

Creative stations kept children engaged with health-themed activities and fun crafts to take home.
