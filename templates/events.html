Teddy Bear Clinic 2026

10:00 AM - 1:00 PM

Join us for our second annual Teddy Bear Clinic! Children can bring their favorite stuffed animals for check-ups, meet local first responders and healthcare professionals, and learn about health, wellness, and safety in a fun, comforting environment.

What to Expect

For Children Ages 3-9

Our event is designed specifically for young children to have fun while learning about healthcare and community helpers.

Free Admission

The Teddy Bear Clinic is completely free for all families. Just bring your favorite stuffed animal!

Food & Refreshments

Light refreshments and snacks will be available in our cafeteria area for all attendees.

Arts & Crafts

Creative stations where children can make health-themed crafts and take home fun memories.

Meet First Responders

Police, Fire, and EMS professionals will be on-site to meet with children and answer questions.

Photo Opportunities

Capture special moments with first responder vehicles, equipment, and your child's teddy bear!

Event Location

Morris County School of Technology

400 E Main St, Denville, NJ 07834

Parking: Free parking available on-site

Accessibility: Fully wheelchair accessible

Weather: Indoor and outdoor activities (rain or shine!)
