Partner With Us

Join us in making a difference for children in our community

Why Partner with Teddy Bear Clinic?

The Teddy Bear Clinic brings together local organizations, schools, businesses, and community groups to create interactive experiences that help children feel comfortable with healthcare and emergency services. By partnering with us, you become part of a meaningful initiative that makes a real impact on families in Morris County and beyond.

Benefits of Partnering

Community Impact

Make a meaningful difference in children's lives by helping reduce fear and anxiety around healthcare and emergency services.

Brand Visibility

Gain exposure to hundreds of families through event promotion, social media features, and on-site recognition at our events.

Networking Opportunities

Connect with other community-minded organizations, businesses, and leaders who share your commitment to child welfare.

Employee Engagement

Provide meaningful volunteer opportunities for your team members to give back and build stronger community connections.

Educational Collaboration

Work alongside healthcare professionals, first responders, and educators in creating interactive learning experiences.

Positive Public Relations

Demonstrate your organization's commitment to community health and youth development through a trusted initiative.

Partnership Opportunities

Event Participation

Set up an interactive booth or activity station at our Teddy Bear Clinic events. Engage directly with children and families while showcasing your organization's services.

Host an Event

Bring the Teddy Bear Clinic experience to your location. Schools, community centers, and organizations can host their own events with our support and guidance.

In-Kind Support

Contribute supplies, materials, or services that help make our events possible. From teddy bears to medical supplies for demonstrations, every contribution counts.

Promotional Partnership

Help spread the word about Teddy Bear Clinic through your networks, newsletters, and social media channels to reach more families in need.

Educational Collaboration

Partner with us to develop educational materials, workshops, or programs that align with our mission of reducing children's healthcare anxiety.
